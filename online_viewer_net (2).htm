Use Case Spotlight

The NetAgent is our AI-Agent, which uses the functionalities and data of the NetOptimizer to speed up your analysis and optimization needs.

Try out the following questions to get a feel for its capabilities:

  • Make me an outage simulation of Router MBRL-DECHE-99-0010! (Follow up with: What is the impact?)
  • Where do I have problems in the network?
  • Give me a custom CSV report with all BTS that don't have uplink traffic.

Future Roadmap

Within the coming months, the NetOptimizer will be extended with:

  • Major Simulation Overhaul: Simulate across network domains and areas with detailed results – all within a new simulation editor.
  • Persistent User Settings: Save your preferences, templates, and simulations effortlessly, independent of your device.
  • SDN Integration: New SDN controllers for MW, IP, and Optical will enhance and extend the NetOptimizer Data Model.
  • NetOptimizer moves to TDL: Our main data source "Columbus DB" is being shifted into the TDL - with all NetOptimizer Reports and Data Exports now accessible from there.

Stay tuned for more!
